Hakeem Olajuwon

Hakeem Abdul olajuwon is a former NBA basket ball player. he was drafted in 1984 and was picked from the Houston Rockets in the number one picked two spots ahead of micheal Jordan. Early on in his career he was a one of a kind player, his footwork which he implamented from playing soccer back in Nigerian helped him a lot from scoring around the basket. He's known as a defensive anchor many fan's call him the 'greatest defender of all time' because his accolades and team achievements showed on what he was done for.

Early Life

Hakeem Olajuwon was born at 1963, January 21st in Lagos Nigeria. As a child hakeem didn't struggle much, his father (Salim) owned a cement business. When he was 15 years old he started to pay attention more on Basketball because he fell in love with it and was gifted because of his height,weight and reach. He got scholarship to play for the university of houston and played all 4 season's. Having one of the best college Basketball careers he was able to be picked number 1 by the Houston Rockets.
